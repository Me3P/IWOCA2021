Program

The program is organized as follows.


The invited talks will be 1 hour live talks presented during the conference.

The contributed paper talks will consist of longer pre-recorded talks (25-30min) available one week before and during the conference for registrants and shorter live talks (<10min) presented during the conference.

Some of these talks, but not all, will become available in the future at the Fields Institute IWOCA 2021 Fields Institute web page.
